Hidden Lake Trail

Location: Glacier National Park, Montana

Difficulty: Easy

Length: 2.7 miles (round trip)

A family-friendly hike offering stunning views of the Hidden Lake and nearby peaks. The well-maintained trail leads through alpine meadows, filled with wildflowers during the summer months. Perfect for beginner hikers or those looking for a scenic, low-impact adventure.

Tips: Bring binoculars for a chance to spot mountain goats and marmots. Start early to avoid the crowds.

Address:
Hidden Lake Trailhead, Logan Pass Visitor Center, West Glacier, MT 59936

Angels Landing

Location: Zion National Park, Utah

Difficulty: Challenging

Length: 5.4 miles (round trip)

Not for the faint of heart, Angels Landing is one of Zion's most iconic and strenuous hikes. The trail includes steep switchbacks, breathtaking views of the canyon, and a narrow ridge with dramatic drop-offs.

Tips: Wear sturdy shoes, bring plenty of water, and be prepared for the heights. The last section is particularly challenging, but the view is worth it.

Address:
The Grotto Trailhead, Zion National Park, Springdale, UT 84767

Cascade Canyon Trail

Location: Grand Teton National Park, Wyoming

Difficulty: Moderate

Length: 9 miles (round trip)

A beautiful, moderate hike that takes you deep into the Tetons. The trail follows Cascade Creek and passes through alpine forests with towering views of the Teton Range. Hikers are treated to waterfalls, wildflowers, and the chance to spot wildlife such as moose and black bears.

Tips: Carry bear spray and stay aware of your surroundings. The trail is especially stunning in late spring when the wildflowers are in bloom.

Address:
Jenny Lake Trailhead, Moose, WY 83012

Lanikai Pillbox Hike

Location: Kailua, Oahu, Hawaii

Difficulty: Moderate

Length: 1.8 miles (round trip)

This short but steep hike offers some of the most spectacular views of Oahu's windward coast. Along the trail, you'll pass WWII-era pillboxes before reaching the summit with breathtaking views of Lanikai Beach below.

Tips: Bring plenty of water and sun protection, as the trail is exposed. It's also a great hike to catch the sunrise if you're up early.

Address:
Lanikai Pillbox Trailhead, Kaelepulu Dr, Kailua, HI 96734

Runyon Canyon

Location: Los Angeles, California

Difficulty: Easy

Length: 3 miles (loop)

A popular trail in the heart of Los Angeles offering sweeping views of the city. The trail is great for leisurely walks or morning jogs, with options for shorter or longer loops.

Tips: Arrive early to avoid crowds. There are also off-leash areas for dogs, making it a fun outing for pet owners.

Address:
Runyon Canyon Trailhead, 2000 N Fuller Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90046
